Creamy Banana Strawberry Split Smoothie

Description

With only 111 calories and 10g protein, this smoothie is the perfect addition to your post workout meal or snack! A delicious sweet treat to enjoy guilt free, with just 12g carbs it is a light drink that will not weigh you down before bedtime.

Ingredients

    1 cup of almond milk
    1 frozen banana, chopped
    3/4 cup of strawberries
    3 ice cubes
    1 scoop of vanilla protein powder
    1 tsp vanilla extract
    1 tsp honey
    1 tsp ground flax seed
    1 tsp ground chia seeds
    1/2 tsp ground cinnamon

Steps

    Combine all of the ingrients into blender
    Blend for 30 seconds or until desired consistency
    Enjoy!
