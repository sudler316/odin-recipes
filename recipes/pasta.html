Pesto Pasta

Description

Taking just 15 minutes total, this pesto pasta is a great go to for busy parents. This meal serves 8 people total once finished! Enjoy it with the family or save it as meal prep throughout the week. Prep is a breeze and the end result is delicious! Serve this classic with french baguettes, cesar salad, and balsamic bruschetta if desired.

Ingredients

  • Pasta shape of your choice
  • Onion
  • Cooking oil
  • Pesto sauce
  • Desired seasonings (or just salt and pepper)
  • Grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water and drain
  2. Cook the oinion in oil, then stir in the pesto and seasonings
  3. Add the pesto mixture to the hot pasta and toss with cheese
